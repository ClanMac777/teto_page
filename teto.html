Kasane Teto (重音テト)

The Diva Born From a Hoax

Kasane Teto is one of the most fascinating figures in the vocal synthesizer community. Often mistaken for an official Vocaloid, she actually has one of the greatest "underdog to superstar" stories in internet history.

"A community-driven virtual singer who evolved from a 2channel April Fools' joke into a cutting-edge AI powerhouse."

1. The April Fools' Origin Story

Teto was originally created in April 2008 as an elaborate April Fools' Day prank on the popular Japanese bulletin board 2channel.

With Hatsune Miku taking the internet by storm at the time, a group of users decided to troll the community by creating a fake "next official Vocaloid" release. They scrambled together character traits, drew a parody design, and had voice provider Mayo Oyamano record a voicebank. When the prank ended, the character had gained so much love that fans refused to let her die. Later that year, her voice was configured for UTAU—a free, user-driven singing software—making her a fully functional community icon.

2. Character Profiles

Because she was created by internet users pitching random ideas, Teto has some of the most oddly specific and hilarious canon lore in the fandom:

  • Age: 31 years old.
  • Gender: Female
  • Voice: Oyamano Mayo
  • Favorite Food: French baguettes.
  • The Hair: Her most defining trait is her twin pigtails shaped like drills.

3. The Massive Tech Upgrade: Synthesizer V

For 15 years, Teto remained a beloved, free community icon primarily hosted on the clunky but charming UTAU engine. However, her status changed entirely in April 2023 when she received an official, commercial upgrade to Synthesizer V AI (in collaboration with TWINDRILL and AH-Software).

This engine uses modern deep-learning technology, transforming Teto from a robotic, choppy virtual software into an incredibly realistic, expressive, and crisp professional vocal powerhouse. Along with her modern voice, she received a sleek outfit redesign, moving to a distinct grey and red military-pop aesthetic.
